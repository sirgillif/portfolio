Programmer, Gamer

I am a 27 years old. I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Central Florida. I spent the 2015-2016 Teaching Geometry at Timber Creek High school. I enjoy everything about computers, taking them apart putting them together, hardware and software. I'm a fanatic gamer, mostly into open word and simulation type gaming, Sim City, City Skylines, etc; any type of game where I can create or influence the world around me. I am currently taking a full stack web development course hosted by the University of Central Florida. In my journey to tinker and learn more about computer, the internet and how it is all created.
